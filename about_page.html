About DCLVP Hobbies


DCLVP Hobbies started off as DCLVP Kicks back in 2019 where we catered only to sneaker collectors.
As time evolved, so did we! Due to demand, we decided to cater to all other hobbies out there and we, too,
share the same passion to all collectors out there.

Founder and CEO Dave Pua has always been fond of hobbies that brought him joy.
Those hobbies included sneakers, collectibles, and trading cards. Those hobbies inspired him to create a one stop shop
for those who share the same interested he had. After 2 years of service, DCLVP Hobbies has created a haven for collectors to come
and hangout and share the same passion Dave had. With this in mind, DCLVP Hobbies is
continuing to provide great and comfortable service to all collectors and won't stop to do so.

Happy Collecting Ka-hobbies!
